Dr Sunil G, has a great educational background with fellowships from NewYork, United Kingdom & Chennai and an outstanding professional experience with numerous surgeries and philanthropical contribution wide spread over rural and urban parts of Karnataka.


He did his MBBS from JJM Medical college, Davangere(1999). Did his MS ophthalmology from the Regional Institute of ophthalmology, Minto Ophthalmic Hospital, Bangalore(2003). Obtained his Diplomate of national board (DNB) certification in 2003 and obtained Fellowship from Royal College of physicians and surgeons (FRCS) from the University of Glasgow, UK (2006). Did his clinical and Research vitreo retinaÂ fellowship from Sankara Nethralya, Chennai. Did his Ratan Tata Phaco fellowship from Sankara Nethralaya, Chennai. Did his fellowship in ocular immunology and uveitis from New york Eye and Ear Infirmary, NewYork, USA. Did his ocular oncology observership in New york Eye and Ear Infirmary, USA. Has presented papers in various international and national conferences around world. Published many articles in peer reviewed index journal .Written many textbook chapters. He is a national level reviewer for many prestigious journals like American journal of ophthalmology (AJO), Journal of American association for pediatric ophthalmology and strabismus (JAAPOS) & British journal of ophthalmology (BJO), Indian journal of ophthalmology.


Dr Sunil G has a very wide professional experience with more than 13000 surgeries. He has a great interests towards charitable activities by performing more than 6000 charity camp surgeries spread over central karnataka. Done many free surgeries to children under Sarvashikshana abhiyana scheme and also children from blind schools with which we reached many rural parts of Karnataka and have worked in upliftment of the people lives. Have conducted many eye problem awareness programmes including eye donation. Being one and only one retinal surgeon from Davangere have done many retinal surgeries at a free of cost and few at very low affordable cost. Conducted eye camps in Hubli, chitradurga and Davangere Districts.


He has very special interests in Vitreo retina, Ocular immunology and ocular oncology. Presently he is a Vitreo retina & Uveitis consultant as well as Chairman Davangere Netralaya Superspecialty Eye Hospital, Davangere, Karnataka. He is the present Chairman Scientific Committee Convener for Karnataka ophthalmic society. Till date he has performed more than 13000 surgeries in Karnataka itself. He has been recognised for his work by International council of ophthalmology and was selected for sponsored training programme to USA out of thousands applicants around the world.
