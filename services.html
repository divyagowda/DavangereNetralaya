What We Offer

Advanced Phacosurgeries

  • NABH approved bio-clean Operation Theaters
  • MICS, Phacoemulsification, Phacosection
  • Premium, toric, monovision & multifocal IOLs
  • IOL workstation: OPD 3 scan, AL /US scans, Specular microscope

Diabetic ARMD & Vitreo-Retinal Service

  • Constellation 23, 25 & 27 G advanced Vitrectomies
  • Iridex Green LASER with SL & IDO delivery
  • Iridex red diode EndoLASER
  • Optovue Macular Spectral OCT
  • Sonomed B scan & Ultrasound Biomicroscopy (UBM)
  • Ophthalmodynamometry
  • HRT 3 Scanning LASER Ophthalmoscopy
  • Anti VEGF Pharmacotherapy

Modern Cornea Grafting Surgeries

  • Government approved centre
  • DALK, DSAEK, DSEK, DMEK, PK etc.
  • Keratoconus clinics with LASIK plus & Intacs
  • Speciality contact lenses
  • ASA & Collagen cross linking CXL/C3R
  • Kerataprosthesis
  • Specular Microscopy
  • Oculus Kerotograph & dry eye workup

Glaucoma, Squint, Pediatric Ophthalmology & Cosmetics

  • OCT, RNFL & GCC, Heidelberg Retinal Tomography III,
  • Computerised Visual Field Analysis, Ultrasound Pachymetry
  • LASER Cyclophotocoagulation, LASER DCR
  • Microsurgical Squint corrections
  • Botox, Blepheroplasty & cosmetic eye surgeries

LASER Vision corrections

  • Wave Front guided Refraction
  • Presbyopia, myopia, hyperopia & astigmatism corrections
  • Thin flap LASIK, No flap ASA/ epiLASIK & FemtoLASER
  • Oculyzer & wavefront guided custom ASA & LASIK plus
  • Custom surface LASER for residual LOA & HOA
  • Vision ICL, IPCL for high myopia
  • Pentacam HR, AS OCT, Topography

Contact Lenses & LVA

  • RGP, standard & customised soft lenses
  • Rose K, Sclera & semiscleral speciality lenses
  • Low vision aids
